Tuna Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Already Prepared Tuna
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Ketchup

Assembly Instructions

  1. Take presliced bread and toast it
  2. Spread prepared tuna on one silce of bread
  3. Place sliced tomato on tuna
  4. Place lettuce on tomato
  5. Cover lettuce with ketchup
  6. Place onion on ketchup
  7. Place last piece of bread on top
  8. Cut into triangles and enjoy

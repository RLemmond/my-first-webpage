Jamon Burre

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Ham
  • pickles(optional)

Assembly Instructions

  1. Slice bread if not already sliced
  2. Toast Bread
  3. Spread butter on Toast
  4. Put ham and pickles on toast
  5. close sandwich and enjoy!
